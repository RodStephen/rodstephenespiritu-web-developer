About Me

On a warm December day in 1997, the Philippines welcomed the arrival of Rod Stephen Espiritu. Little did the world know that this day marked the beginning of a remarkable journey, one that would be characterized by love and joy that would shape the world.

Born in the heart of the Philippines, Rod Stephen or you can call him Rod for short has spent his formative years surrounded by the rich culture, warm hospitality, and natural beauty.

In the tapestry of Rod's life, the chapter of love and companionship shines brightly. He found a soulmate in Erica and embarked on the journey of marriage, creating a partnership grounded in mutual respect, shared dreams, and unwavering support. The story of Rod and Erica is a testament to the strength found in unity.

Renowned for his unyielding work ethic and passion, Rod is recognized as a hardworking and passionate individual. In the realm of software programming, he embody a relentless spirit of curiosity and a dedication to continuous learning. This unwavering commitment to personal and professional growth paints a vivid picture of Rod's character.
